You will add a colleague as an **Admin** on your app so they can edit it, view statistics, and share it with others — while keeping the app **Private** for external users.

## Prerequisites

- You are the **Owner** of the app. Only owners can add admins.
- Your teammate already has an [OnMicro.AI](https://onmicro.ai) account registered with the email address you will enter.
- The app open in the editor, or available from your [dashboard](https://onmicro.ai/dashboard).

Looking to share with learners or the public instead? See [Share your app](/tutorials/share-your-app).

## Step 1 — Open the Share tab

1. Open your app in the editor.
2. Click **Share** in the center navigation bar.

Scroll to **People with access** at the bottom of the **Share** tab. You should see yourself listed as **Owner**.

## Step 2 — Keep the app private

For internal collaboration, leave **Who can access** set to **Private** — *You and admins only*.

Private means:

- You and admins can open and edit the app when logged in.
- External users cannot run the app, even with the direct link.

Admins do not need the app to be **Public**. If the app is already public, admins still get edit access — but anyone with the link can also use it. Set **Private** if the app is for your team only.

## Step 3 — Add an admin by email

1. In **People with access**, type your teammate's OnMicro.AI account email in **Add someone by email…**.
2. Click **Add**.

**Checkpoint:** The person appears in the list with the **Admin** role next to their name.

Common messages if adding fails:

| Message | What it means |
|---------|----------------|
| *No account found with that email address.* | They need to sign up first, or you used the wrong email. |
| *This user already has access to the app.* | They are already an owner or admin. |
| *This app has reached the maximum of 25 admins.* | Remove an admin before adding another. |

Adding admins is **owner-only**. Admins can see the **People with access** list but cannot add or remove other people.

## Step 4 — Confirm your teammate has access

Ask your teammate to sign in and check their dashboard:

1. In the left sidebar, click **Shared with me**.
2. The app should appear in the list.
3. They can click the title or **Edit** to open the **Build** tab.

Admins can also use **Preview**, **Share**, **Statistics**, and **Clone** (if cloning is allowed). They cannot **Delete** the app — only the owner can.

## Step 5 — Collaborate

Admins have the same editing tools as the owner:

- Change components on the **Build** tab.
- Use **App Builder** to modify the app in chat.
- Adjust **App settings** and view **Statistics**.

Changes save automatically for everyone with access. Coordinate who is editing to avoid overwriting each other's work.

## Remove access

### Owner removes an admin

1. Open **Share** → **People with access**.
2. Hover over the admin's row.
3. Click the trash icon to remove them.

They lose access immediately and the app disappears from their **Shared with me** list.

### Admin leaves on their own

1. On the [dashboard](https://onmicro.ai/dashboard), find the app under **Shared with me**.
2. Hover the app card and click **Leave**.
3. Confirm when prompted.

## What you have now

Your teammate has **Admin** access to a private app. They can edit and monitor the app without exposing it to external users.
